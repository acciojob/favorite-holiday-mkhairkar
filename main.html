Ladakh


Ladakh, the "Land of High Passes" in North India, is a breathtaking union territory known as a high-altitude cold desert...

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Pangong Lake: The famous blue lake.
  2. Nubra Valley: Home to the sand dunes.
  3. Tso Moriri Lake: A high-altitude sanctuary.
  4. Leh Palace: A historic royal residence.
  5. Magnetic Hill & Sangam: Where gravity defies logic.

Must-Try Foods

  • Momos – Steamed or fried dumplings.
  • Skyu – A traditional thumb-sized pasta soup.
  • Thukpa – Hearty noodle soup.
  • Khambir – Traditional Ladakhi local bread.
  • Chhutagi – A local delicacy shaped like a bow-tie.
