Ladakh


Ladakh, the "Land of High Passes" in North India, is a breathtaking union territory known as a high-altitude cold desert, bordering Tibet, Himachal Pradesh, and Pakistan-administered Kashmir. Featuring Leh and Kargil districts, it offers rugged mountain beauty, Tibetan Buddhist culture, and stunning lakes like Pangong.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Pangong Lake
  2. Nubra Valley
  3. Tso Moriri Lake
  4. Leh Palace
  5. Magnetic Hill & Sangam

Must-Try Foods

  • Momos
  • Skyu
  • Thukpa
  • Khambir
  • Chhutagi
